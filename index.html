About

Hi there!

My name is Aisling McCallion, I'm from Donegal in Ireland. I am currently living and studying in Limerick City.

I am currently undertaking a Masters degree in Interaction & Experience Design at the University of Limerick.

I graduated from Fashion, Knitwear & Textile Design in 2017 from the Limerick School of Art & Design. From 2017 to 2019 I lived and worked as a Knitwear Designer & Shima Seiki Programmer on Inis Me√°in, the middle island of the Aran Islands. I developed a strong interest in heritage, fashion technology and nature in design while inhabiting the island. In 2019, I completed a Minor Award in Social Enterprise from the University of Limerick. During this year I was also a member of spacecraft Artist's Studios and became Co-director of the space in the beginning of 2020. At the studio, I developed a freelance knintwear consultant business while exploring potential new avenues for my journey with textiles. In August 2020, I moved to Kilcar in Co.Donegal. Here under the employment of Blarney Woollen Mills, I created a new brand for the factory in Kilcar, Baye. The project let me realise the accumination of textile knowledge and skills I had gained from learning to handknit at a young age from my grandmother.

This experience also pushed me to realise that my moral values of sustainabilty, circular design and partipartoy design were incompatibale with a tradtionalist fashion system. Eager to re-position myself, re-skill and further study design methodologies, I embarked on my masters journey.

I maintain a writing practice and engage with textiles through self-directed research and enjoyment!
